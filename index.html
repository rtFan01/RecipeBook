Recipe Spotlight: Grilled Cheese

This is one of my personal favorites, grilled cheese! I use a seeded sourdough baguette, but any baguette should do.

Serves: 2 - 3 people
Time: 2 - 4 minutes set-up, 10 - 15 minutes cook
Tools: A frying pan, a cutting board, a stove, a bread knife, and a butter/cheese knife

Ingredients Amount
Baguette 1
Cheese 1/2 a block, depending
Butter 1/5 stick
  1. Cut up cheese and baguette into similarly-sized slices
  2. Butter, or otherwise grease, and heat the pan on low - medium
  3. Make small sandwiches with the baguette and cheese slices
  4. Butter the outside of the sandwiches
  5. Put sandwiches in the pan
  6. Let cook for 5 - 7 minutes, then flip
  7. After a similar amount of time has passed, turn off the stove and let cool
  8. Serve

Recipe Spotlight: Potatoes

This is another favorite of mine. I use russet potatoes, but any type should do.

Serves: 2 - 3 people
Time: 15 - 20 minutes set-up, 10 - 15 minutes cook
Tools: A frying pan, a cutting board, a stove, a knife that can chop and mince, and a wooden spoon

Ingredients Amount
Potatoes 3 medium
Garlic 4 - 6 segments
Olive Oil As much as needed
Scallions 2 - 3
Salt As much as needed
  1. Cut potatoes into fry-like shapes
  2. Mince garlic and chop scallions
  3. Grease pan with olive oil, and turn stove on low - medium heat
  4. Add potatoes, garlic, and scallions
  5. Add olive oil and salt as needed
  6. Let cook for 10 - 15 minutes; don't forget to taste test!
  7. Let cool
  8. Serve
