Grilled Cheese

Serves: 2 - 3 people
Time: 2 - 4 minutes set-up, 10 - 15 minutes cook
Tools: A frying pan, a cutting board, a stove, a bread knife, and a butter/cheese knife

Ingredients Amount
Baguette 1
Cheese 1/2 a block, depending
Butter 1/5 stick
  1. Cut up cheese and baguette into similarly-sized slices
  2. Butter, or otherwise grease, and heat the pan on low - medium
  3. Make small sandwiches with the baguette and cheese slices
  4. Butter the outside of the sandwiches
  5. Put sandwiches in the pan
  6. Let cook for 5 - 7 minutes, then flip
  7. After a similar amount of time has passed, turn off the stove and let cool
  8. Serve
